How It Works

Report the Problem

Take a photo of the issue and provide details about the location and nature of the problem. Your report helps us understand what needs attention.

Track Status Updates

Monitor the progress of your report in real-time. Receive updates as our team reviews, assigns, and works on resolving the issue.

Get Notified When Resolved

Receive instant notifications when your reported issue has been resolved. See the difference you've made in your community.
